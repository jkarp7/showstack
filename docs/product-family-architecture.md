# ShowStack Product Family Architecture
## Strategic Naming & Module Design Proposal

**Created:** December 29, 2025
**Purpose:** Define the complete ShowStack product family with discipline-specific modules
**Status:** Strategic Proposal

---

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**Current State:** ShowStack:Production is a lighting-focused tool competing with LightWright 6, but the name "Production" is too broad and doesn't clearly communicate the lighting focus.

**Proposed State:** Restructure ShowStack into a **modular product family** where each entertainment discipline gets its own dedicated module with clear naming:

- **ShowStack:Lighting** (formerly Production) - For lighting designers & electricians
- **ShowStack:Sound** (new) - For sound designers & A1s
- **ShowStack:Video** (future) - For video/projection designers
- **ShowStack:Production** (renamed scope) - For production managers & stage managers
- **ShowStack:Tour** (formerly Manager) - For tour managers & coordinators
- **ShowStack:Producer** (future) - For producers & general managers

### Why This Makes Sense

✅ **Clarity**: Each module name clearly indicates its discipline/role
✅ **Scalability**: Easy to add new discipline modules over time
✅ **Marketing**: Clearer value proposition for each target audience
✅ **Pricing Flexibility**: Can price each module independently with bundles
✅ **Professional Alignment**: Matches how the industry organizes departments
✅ **Cross-sell Opportunities**: Natural upsell paths between modules

## 📦 Proposed ShowStack Product Family

### 1. ShowStack:Lighting (formerly Production)
**Target Users:** Lighting Designers, Associate LDs, Production Electricians, Master Electricians

**Core Features:**
- Virtual spreadsheet for fixtures (10,000+ row performance)
- Dimmer & power distribution management
- DMX mapping and addressing
- Circuit management & phase balancing
- Multi-cable tracking (Socapex, breakouts)
- Vectorworks integration & reconciliation
- ETC Eos console integration (OSC)
- Label designer & printing (Dymo, Brother, Zebra)
- Custom paperwork generation (instrument schedule, channel hookup, dimmer hookup, etc.)
- Error checking & auto-fix suggestions
- Color cut lists & gobo tracking

**Direct Competitors:**
- LightWright 6 ($845)
- Vectorworks (limited paperwork capabilities)

**Pricing:**
- Professional: $249/year
- Student: $60/year
- Institutional: $399/seat/year (min 10 seats)

## 🎯 Strategic Advantages of Modular Architecture

### 1. **Clear Market Positioning**
- Each module has a specific, well-defined competitor
- ShowStack:Lighting vs LightWright 6
- ShowStack:Sound vs Minotaur
- ShowStack:Video vs (nothing - greenfield!)
- ShowStack:Production vs manual tools
- ShowStack:Tour vs manual tools
- ShowStack:Producer vs Tessitura/manual tools

### 2. **Flexible Pricing & Revenue Optimization**
- Users only pay for what they need
- Natural upsell paths (start with one module, add more)
- Bundle discounts encourage multi-module adoption
- Higher lifetime value as users add modules over time

### 3. **Reduced Barriers to Entry**
- Lower initial price point ($199-299 vs $999)
- Easier to justify department-specific budget
- Can expand usage as value is proven

### 4. **Targeted Marketing**
- Discipline-specific messaging
- Attend specialized conferences (lighting, sound, video separately)
- Partner with discipline-specific organizations
- Content marketing tailored to each role

### 5. **Development Flexibility**
- Can prioritize modules based on market demand
- Iterate on one module without affecting others
- Release cadence can vary by module maturity
- Beta test modules independently

### 6. **Competitive Differentiation**
- **Only platform offering unified cross-department solution**
- LightWright is lighting-only
- Minotaur is sound-only
- No one offers video-specific tools
- No one offers production/tour/producer modules

### 7. **Cross-sell & Network Effects**
- Lighting designer introduces sound designer colleague → new customer
- Student learns on multiple modules → professional customer for life
- Production manager needs visibility into all departments → complete bundle
- Institutions want unified platform → institutional bundle

## 💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

### ✅ Strong YES - Rename Production → Lighting

**Reasons:**
1. **Clarity:** "Lighting" immediately communicates the scope
2. **Positioning:** Directly positions against LightWright 6
3. **Scalability:** Enables clear modular expansion
4. **Marketing:** Easier to explain and market
5. **Professional alignment:** Matches how the industry works

### ✅ Strong YES - Modular Product Family

**Reasons:**
1. **Revenue optimization:** Bundle discounts drive higher LTV
2. **Market coverage:** Can compete in multiple niches
3. **Cross-sell:** Natural expansion paths
4. **Reduced risk:** Don't need all modules to succeed
5. **Competitive moat:** Only unified platform in entertainment

### 🎯 Recommended Prioritization

**Year 1:** Lighting (rename + complete)
**Year 2:** Sound (high demand, clear competitor)
**Year 3:** Video (greenfield!) + Production (strategic)
**Year 4:** Tour + Producer (specialized, higher price)

### 🚨 Risks & Mitigation

**Risk:** Development complexity across multiple modules
**Mitigation:** Shared platform team, modular architecture

**Risk:** Confusing pricing with too many options
**Mitigation:** Clear bundle recommendations, calculator tool

**Risk:** Support complexity across disciplines
**Mitigation:** Discipline-specific support specialists

**Risk:** Brand dilution
**Mitigation:** Strong ShowStack brand, consistent UX
